Dum Briyani

Description

Biryani is a rich and flavorful South Asian rice dish made with aromatic basmati rice, fragrant spices, and a choice of meat (like chicken, mutton), seafood, or vegetables. Originating from the Indian subcontinent, it is often layered and cooked using the dum (slow-steaming) method, allowing the flavors to infuse deeply. Biryani is celebrated for its irresistible aroma, colorful presentation, and perfect blend of spices.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Rice
  • Chicken
  • Briyani Masala
  • Lemon
  • Curd
  • Cooking Oil
  • onion

Steps

  1. Wash Rice & Chicken and make them ready
  2. Boil Rice and Fry Chicken & onion
  3. Put curd in fried chicken
  4. Put Rice in a Pot followed by chicken, cooking oil, Briyani Masala, and Fried onion
  5. Put the lemon
  6. Pack the Pot and Dum for 15 min.
    7. And now you're ready with Dum Briyani.
