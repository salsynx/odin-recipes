Tea

Description

Tea is a popular aromatic beverage made by steeping cured or fresh tea leaves in hot water. It can be served plain or flavored with milk, sugar, lemon, or spices, depending on regional preferences. It’s known for its soothing taste and refreshing properties.

Ingredients

  • Water – 1 cup (250 ml)
  • Tea leaves / tea bag – 1 tsp loose tea or 1 tea bag
  • Milk (optional) – ¼ to ½ cup
  • Sugar (optional) – 1 to 2 tsp

Steps

  1. Boil Water: In a saucepan, add 1 cup of water and bring it to a boil.
  2. Add Tea Leaves: Add 1 tsp of tea leaves (or 1 tea bag). Let it simmer for 1–2 minutes.
  3. Add Milk: Add ¼ to ½ cup of milk, depending on how strong or light you want it.
  4. Add Sugar: Stir in sugar to taste.
  5. Boil Again: Let the mixture boil for 2–3 more minutes.
  6. Strain and Serve: Strain the tea into a cup and serve hot
